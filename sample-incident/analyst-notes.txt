            Rule name: "Archive-Rule-Auto" (designed to blend in).

SUMMARY — ANALYST ASSESSMENT

Root cause: Successful credential phishing via spoofed DocuSign email.
            Legacy auth MFA exemption allowed attacker to bypass MFA.

Impact confirmed:
  - 1 account compromised (k.thornton)
  - ~40MB data exfiltrated (finance, HR, vendor contracts)
  - 3 emails forwarded to external address (contents: sensitive internal data)
  - Wire fraud attempted: $47,500. Payment blocked by bank hold.
  - GDPR notification required (EU employee compensation data accessed)

Impact avoided:
  - Wire transfer blocked ($47,500 saved)
  - No lateral movement confirmed
  - No ransomware deployment

Dwell time: ~1 hour 39 minutes (09:12 initial access → 10:51 internal escalation → 10:58 containment)

Estimated cost: Legal fees + notification ~$15,000–$25,000. Wire fraud avoided = $47,500 saved.

Open items:
  - ICO notification by 2026-03-14 13:00 UTC
  - Phishing awareness training for all finance/AP staff
  - Legacy auth exemption process review
  - Email security gateway rule for lookalike domains
